About Me

I am a researcher at NEC Labs America. I received my PhD at Seoul National University in 2019, advised by Kyoung Mu Lee. Before joining NEC, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Seoul National University, advised by Bohyung Han and Kyoung Mu Lee. My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning.
